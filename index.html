About

I am a Professor in the Departamento de Ciencias Matemáticas y Físicas at the Universidad Católica de Temuco (Chile). Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher in the group of Dr. Sander Rhebergen and Dr. David Del Rey Fernández in the Department of Applied Mathematics at the University of Waterloo (Canada). I obtained my PhD from the Centro de Investigación en Ingeniería Matemática (CI²MA) at the Universidad de Concepción (Chile) under the supervision of Dr. Manuel Solano and Dr. Ricardo Oyarzúa.

Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of Numerical Analysis of Partial Differential Equations and Scientific Computing. I have a particular interest in the analysis of finite element and discontinuous Galerkin methods, and the development of a posteriori error estimators.
